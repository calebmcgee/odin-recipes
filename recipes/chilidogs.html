Hotdogs

Description

Quick, easy way to make a 2 hotdog meal.

Use when in need of food but the fridge is almost empty.

Ingredients

  • 2 uncooked hotdogs
  • 2 hotdog buns
  • chili
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 and place foil on a baking sheet
  2. Put hotdogs on baking sheet, put into oven. Cook thouroghly, flipping hotdogs as needed
  3. While hotdogs are in oven, heat chili in saucepan at medium heat, and put hotdog buns in toaster.
  4. When hotdogs are cooked through, put them in the buns on a plate, add desired amount of chili and cheese on top.
