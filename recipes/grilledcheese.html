Grilled Cheese Sandwhich

Description

Grilled Cheese recipe

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • 2 peices of White bread
  • Shredded Mexican Blend Cheese

Steps

  1. Make sandwhich on a plate by getting the white bread and adding cheese as needed
  2. Place pan on stove at medium heat, butter pan as necessary
  3. When butter is melted, add Oregano and basil, then place sandwhich on pan.
  4. Cook until brown, flip and repeat. Add butter if needed after flipping.
